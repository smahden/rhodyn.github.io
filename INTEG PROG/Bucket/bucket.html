My Bucket List


Concert Tour

One of my dreams is to be able to tour the world along with of my favorite artists. Having the chance to explore other places while seeing them perform there is a one of a kind experience.



E-sports Event

To see the best of the best duke it out in a big arena with thousands of people watching isn't comparable to watching it behind the screen. I am hoping to experience being a live viewer at least once or if God allows, to actually stand in the very same stage they compete on.



Hiking

A walk, for me, is the simplest form of adventure. What better way to elevate the walking experience than by going to such heights like the mountains for example.



Travelling

To see different sceneries, to experience a different culture. We all have our reasons to want to travel, and those also stay true to me. One of my life goals is to travel to at least 5 countries.
