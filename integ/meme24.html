Filipinos in the summer

ORIGIN

2022


DESCRIPTION

It has been common for people to seek cold things when experiencing hot weather. But not for Filipinos, The culture of Filipinos has normalized seeing people seeking a creamy hot coffee even at the hottest temperatures. Some believe that people crave the caffeine it gives off or just love the taste in general. Hence, Seeing numerous coffee shops scattered around the Philippines. Also the character shown in the image holding a coffee in the summer is Nanami Kento from Jujutsu Kaisen
