“There’s only thing worse than a Boomer… BOOM!; A Filipino”

ORIGIN

2022


DESCRIPTION

The meme origination started from a screenshot of a clip in a scene in a youtube video named “NEIGHBORHOOD PATROL Episode 2: Pedo Hunt” made by the youtuber named Brandon Rogers. The original had a different text where it started as ““There’s only thing worse than a pedophile… BOOM!; A Child; NO!”. But with the power of photoshop the editor was able to change the text. The context of the meme is how some people get triggered by the words of our elderly Filipino people, where they pursue touchy topics and entitlement of respect etc.
